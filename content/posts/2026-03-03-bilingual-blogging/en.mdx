One of the core requirements for this blog was supporting both English and Roman Urdu.

## The Strategy

I decided to use a folder-based structure where `en.mdx` acts as the source of truth for metadata.

- `en.mdx`: Contains the YAML frontmatter and English content.
- `ur.mdx`: Contains only the content.

This ensures that tags, dates, and excerpts are consistent across both versions.
